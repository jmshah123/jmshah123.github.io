Requirements in Kali Chaturdashi 'Pooja'

This pooja can be performed only by male members or by a brahmin at Bhurakhia require the following
 
Ladoo made-up of following
 
Offerings required Quantity
 
1 Bread (rotla) Four sher and six tolas
 
2 Ghee One and half sher
 
3 Jaggery One sher
 
A coconut (shreefal) is to be offered in the evening in the North-West corner of the house
